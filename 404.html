Our story

Kranz Wolfe Associates (KWA) started in 2018, an advisory company in supply chain with main focus on products of Dassault Systèmes (3DS).

Our vision is based on three principles:

  • Service based expertise
  • No nonsense
  • Commitment

KWA executes projects while keeping overhead under control and full focus on value.

KWA has extensive supply chain experience and technical know how to help you on your supply chain puzzle.

Our mission

Kranz Wolfe Associates is recognized as a key player in delivery of products and services in the Dassault Systèmes ecosphere.

Our services

Kranz Wolfe Associates (KWA) guides you in your project journey. From Value Scan and Business Analysis to Process Optimization and Implementation, our experience supports you to increase speed and value.
